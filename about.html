About Brandon Wong


Brandon Wong is an accomplished origami designer and artist with over 100 recorded original designs. His design style features intricate and novel structural techniques, while keeping the balance between art and engineering. Recently, his online content under the handle @ThePlantPsychologist has gained popularity and has reached millions of viewers.

Originally from the San Francisco Bay Area, Brandon is currently studying mechanical engineering at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). His research work includes computational origami under the mentorship of Prof. Erik Demaine, as well as independent research within the field of origami design, and has two first-authored publications in the field.

Additionally, Brandon has served as the president of MIT's OrigaMIT club since 2021, and has spearheaded the conventions for the past few years. He is also involved with Origami USA and has taught at many in-person and online events.
