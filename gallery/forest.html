Forest

August 2020

Designed right after folding the Ryujin 3.5, and scales were still fresh in my mind. Naturally, when I returned to the problem of creating trees, I thought of using a scale-like pattern as a leaf texture.

Each corner of the cp is one of the 4 trees, with two small trees on top and two big trees at the bottom.

Grid: 56x56, with partial 112ths for the leaf pleats.
