Stanford Bunny

November 2023

The "Stanford bunny" is a 3d shape often used as a test object for rendering softwares or other things that are supposed to be able to operate on arbitrarily complex 3d geometries. I wanted to demonstrate hex pleating's capabilities for 3d objects by creating a model of the Stanford bunny.
