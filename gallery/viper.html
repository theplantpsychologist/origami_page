Viper

January 2025

A new hex pleated snake, with an entirely new diamond-shaped scale pattern. Although a huge pain to fold (the whole model took over 30 hours), these scales and other hex-pleating-specific tricks are the secret to this model's extremely high efficiency. The model was folded from a 2x3 rectangle, and from tongue to tail measures around 80% of the paper's long side. For reference, ryujin 3.5 as well as my previous hp snake are also around 80% efficiency, but this one only needs 2/3rds of the paper. Could these scales be used for a ryujin 4.0? Only time will tell.
