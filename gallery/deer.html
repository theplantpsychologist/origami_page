Deer Couple

January 2022

Using human anatomy tricks to create gendered animal figures, such as neck and shoulder width.

The cp on the left is the male; the reference line (green) is the intersection of a 22.5 crease with a crease of slope 3. The cp on the right is the female, using the "Kamiya reference".

To keep the model proportions with one another, fold the female with a slightly smaller sheet of paper.
