Friends

January 2022

One main challenge with human figure design is creating emotional expressions. Here, the main feature that makes the figures "smile" are the raised cheeks under their eyes.

This multi-figure structure uses the same technique as used in Family. Technically, this would be called multiaxial box pleating, with one axis for each figure. The friends are literally inseparable (joined from shoulder to heel), and that greatly improves the model's efficiency.

Grid: 40x40
