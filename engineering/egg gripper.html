Compliant egg gripper

Completed November 2024

Collaborators: Andy Dequin, Alexandra Valdepeñas, Brooke Rethman, Jose Betances

For 2.145 (Design of compliant mechanisms), made from a waterjet sheet of acrylic. The gripper is designed as one solid piece, acheiving the motion through flexures. It also features flexures to pad the egg.
